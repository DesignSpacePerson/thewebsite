RealSuite Redesign

I am currently leading the UX/UI Design Department at BGIS North America, which hosts the largest traffic of daily activity amongst our clients, vendors, and users alike, who use our asset management SaaS platforms. I am grateful for this opportunity that has not only allowed me to define BGIS' Design System and overall direction in User Experience across our flagship product, RealSuite, but with it, I have established relationships within numerous departments. This allows us to symbiotically work alongside one another to acheive incredible results for our clients worldwide.

Within just a few short years, I have been able to push accessibility assessments as a standard in our processes and is included in our foundational designs and builds from the get go. There is also multi-language support, and with the help of various departments, my team provides governance across all of our new and existing products to ensure that we are able to provide consistently usable experiences althroughout.

For the privacy and safety of our clients, as well as due to non-disclosure restrictions, I am unable to reveal any visuals or further details about our work here.
