Guile Domingo is just a very curious, yet grateful human being

He firmly believes in becoming a better version of himself, in the unexpected kindness of others, and that the curly fry at the bottom of the bag of straight fries is never there by accident, but due to the generosity of unsung heroes

UX-ing Offline,
All The Time

Guile's curiosity goes far beyond the four boundaries of his phone's screen, yet it's crazy to him that he keeps finding himself back to those four walls. It's fascinating, isn't it? A whole world before you that you now hold at the palm of your hand. Yes, this kind of power can be dangerous, but it also holds the key to a better tomorrow, whatever that could mean for you and the billions of others staring at these same screens in this exact moment.

As daunting or naive as that notion may be, that's exactly what excites a guy like Guile. The potential for positive, impactful moments that can be done with whatever's at the palm of your hands today; isn't that worth exploring?

No matter how minute nor complex the challenge may be, he is always happy to lend an ear over a cup of tea.
